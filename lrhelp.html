Login/Register Help

How to Register

If on the 'Sign-In' page locate and click the 'Register' button to be taken to the Register page. Locate the 'Register Page' box and fill out the following fields: First Name, Last Name, Phone Number, Email, Password, Type. Then click the 'Register' button.

How to Login

Locate the 'Sign-In' box and fill out the following fields: Email, Password, Type. Then click the 'Login' button.
