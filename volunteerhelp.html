Volunteer Help

How to Sign-up for an Appointment

Locate the 'Display Sign-up' box and fill out the 'Date' field for the appointment and click the 'Display' button.

How to Look Up My Appointments

Locate the 'My Calendar' box and fill out the 'Date' field, then click the 'Display' button to view your appointments on the date entered.

How to Logout

Go to the top right corner and click 'logout'.
