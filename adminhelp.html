Admin Help

How to Look Up Volunteers

Locate the 'Search Volunteers' box and fill out the 'Search' field and choose either Email, First or Last to decide how the volunteer will be searched. Then click the 'Display' button.

How to Look Up Owners

Locate the 'Search Owners' box and fill out the 'Search' field and choose either Email, First or Last to decide how the owner will be searched. Then click the 'Display' button.

How to Look Up Appointments

Locate the 'Search Requests' box and fill out the 'Date' field OR 'Email' field. Then click the 'Display' button. ~DO NOT FILL OUT BOTH FIELDS~

How to Logout

Go to the top right corner and click 'logout'.
